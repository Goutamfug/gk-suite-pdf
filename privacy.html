Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. No Data Collection

PDF Master Suite Pro runs entirely in your browser. No servers receive your files. No PDFs or personal data are ever uploaded.

2. Local Processing Only

All PDF operations happen on your device. Your data never leaves your computer.

3. Optional Account (Local Storage)

If you create an account, your name and email are stored only in your browser's localStorage. No backend database exists.

4. Camera & Microphone Access

Optional features request permissions only when used. Images and audio are never transmitted to any server.

5. No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies.

Your trust is important to us. We built this Tool with privacy as the #1 priority.
